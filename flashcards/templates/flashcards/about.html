About this webpage

This page is originally written for fun and educational purpose, aimed to help myself getting started on recognizing characters in Asian (specifically Buddhist) calligrapy. When I showed it to people around me I received enthusiastic reactions so I decided to improve usability and keep it online.

The inspiration for making this page came entirely from reading Heart of the Brush: The Splendor of East Asian Calligraphy by Kaz Tanahashi. The book displays wonderful calligraphy based on 150 characters, which formed the curriculum for this page.
For beginning to learn reading Chinese characters, I would recommend Chineasy: The New Way to Read Chinese by the wonderful ShaoLan Hsueh.

This page is written and maintained by Maarten Fugers. If you have questions or comments, please send me a message on LinkedIn!
